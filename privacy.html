Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026  ·  Applies to all Hatmaker Apps Android applications

🔒

The short version: Hatmaker Apps games do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from any user. Our apps run entirely on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no data sent to us or anyone else.

01 About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Android applications developed and published by Hatmaker Apps (a brand of JEH Electronics) on the Google Play Store, including: Hangman, Sink It!, and Code Breaker, as well as any future applications we release.

By downloading or using any of our applications, you agree to the terms described in this policy. JEH Electronics is based in Idaho, USA.

02 Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Period.

Our applications do not collect, request, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact identifiers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware identifiers
  • Usage data, analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Contacts, calendar data, or communications

Any game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

03 Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or data-collection services of any kind.

Please note that the Google Play Store may collect information about your device and app usage in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This is governed entirely by Google and is outside our control.

04 Permissions

If any of our applications request Android permissions, those permissions are used solely to provide on-device functionality. We do not use permissions to collect, store, or transmit personal data. Specific permission details are listed in each app's Google Play Store listing.

05 Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for the whole family, including young children. Because we collect absolutely no personal data from any user, our apps are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

06 Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal data on our servers to protect. Game data stored locally on your device is subject to the security features of your Android device and operating system.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of our applications after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If we ever make a material change — such as beginning to collect personal data in a future app — we will provide clear notice within the affected application and update this policy accordingly.
